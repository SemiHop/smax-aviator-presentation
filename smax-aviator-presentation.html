ü§ñ Aviator Agents: Autonomous Task Managers

What are Aviator Agents?

AI-powered bots that can complete tasks autonomously. Think of them as specialized workers who take over a task when they are called.

  • Generative AI actions execution
  • Process automation and integrations
  • Human-in-the-loop steps supported
  • Operates independently once triggered

üîç Aviator Agents vs. AI-Enriched Workflows

AI-Enriched Workflows: AI is embedded directly into a workflow - enhancing a process with AI intelligence.
Aviator Agents: Tasks are handed off to an independent AI agent that operates autonomously - like delegating to a specialized worker.
